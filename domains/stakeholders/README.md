# Stakeholders

Stakeholder notes track relationships, influence, interests, and communication preferences
for people and teams outside your immediate organization.

## When to Create a Stakeholder Note

- You interact with them regularly about your team's work
- They have decision-making authority over your backlog or roadmap
- They consume your team's services or APIs
- They influence funding, staffing, or priority decisions

## Template

```markdown
# {Stakeholder Name}

## Role & Influence
- Title/Role:
- Organization:
- Decision authority: (approver / influencer / informed)
- Key interests:

## Communication Preferences
- Preferred channel: (email / Teams / meeting)
- Frequency: (weekly / biweekly / ad-hoc)
- Style notes: (concise vs. detailed, data-driven vs. narrative)

## Active Topics
- {Topic 1}: {status}
- {Topic 2}: {status}

## History
- {Date}: {Key interaction or decision}
```

## Naming Convention

`{name}.md` — lowercase, hyphenated. Example: `stakeholders/program-board.md`
