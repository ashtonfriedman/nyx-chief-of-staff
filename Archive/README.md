# Archive

Completed or inactive material, preserved but out of the way.

## What Goes Here

- Initiatives that shipped or were abandoned
- Domain notes for people who left or teams that reorganized
- Expertise docs superseded by newer versions
- Anything you don't need every session but might reference someday

## Conventions

- Move, don't delete. The archive preserves institutional memory.
- Keep the original folder structure: `Archive/initiatives/old-project/`
- Add a one-line note at the top: `> Archived {date}: {reason}`
